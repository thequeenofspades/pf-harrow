How to Use

Click to generate a new harrow spread of 9 cards. Hover over a card to see its description. If the card is misaligned in the spread, its misaligned description will be shown below the ordinary interpretation.

Click to copy a player-safe URL to your clipboard. This URL will not show descriptions for the cards when they are hovered.

A card displayed upside-down is misaligned.

A card with a blue glow is a True Match.

A card with a white glow is a Partial Match.

A card with a red glow is an Opposite Match.
